FAQ
Until what grade does Mzamotsha Primary School operate?

While Mzamomtsha started as a primary school from Grade 1-7, our school also now accommodates Gr 8-9.

Do we have to come onto the school site to enroll our child?

We are happy to inform parents that Mzamomtsha Primary School has implemented an online enrollment form for parents and students unable to make it onsite for registration.

What events does the school host?

Mzamomtsha hosts a variety of fun and educational events throughout the school year, please navigate to the events pae on this website to find out more.

What are Mzamomtsha Primary School's operating hours?

Mzamomtsha Primary School's operating hours are Mon-Fri, from 08:00 - 17:00.

How can we get ahold of Mzamotsha Primary School?

Mzamomtsha can be contacted via email, phone and the form on the website's 'Contact Us' page.
