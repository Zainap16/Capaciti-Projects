1)In your group, brainstorm the following questions and capture your answers
 for the other groups to see:

=What is a proposal?

-A proposal is usually a suggestion, often written, to get people
 to support a plan/idea.

=What is a funding proposal?

-A request for funding that businesses submit to key stakeholders.

=Why is a funding proposal needed?

-Too convince key stakeholders that your project is worthy of funding.

=Then, briefly share your answers with the other groups.
