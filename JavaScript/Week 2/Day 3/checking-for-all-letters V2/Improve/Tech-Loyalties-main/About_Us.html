About Us

Our vision at Mzamomtsha Primary School is to empower students to acquire, demonstrate, articulate and value knowledge and skills that will support them, as life-long learners, to participate in and contribute to the global world and practice the core values of the school: respect, tolerance & inclusion, and excellence.

Our mission is to develop students with active and creative minds, a sense of understanding and compassion for others, and the courage to act on their beliefs. We stress the total development of each child: spiritual, moral, intellectual, social, emotional, and physical.

About Our Principle!

From his days in junior high, our headmaster, Matt Pollock was strongly influenced by the ministry of Christian School education. His father was a teacher, coach, and administrator in a Christian school, and he demonstrated the transformational impact that could be experienced by students in this setting. Matt became convinced that God wanted him to serve in this field and so he continued his studies at Clarks Summit University (formerly named Baptist Bible College) in northeastern Port Elizabeth. He graduated with a bachelor's degree in Secondary Education with an emphasis in Bible and History. His love of sports pushed him to also develop his skills in coaching during his college years as well.
